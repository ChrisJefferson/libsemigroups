Classsemigroupsplusplus::TropicalMinPlusSemiring

Tropical min-plus semiring.

The tropical min-plus semiring consists of the integers 0, ... , t for some value t (called the threshold of the semiring) and infinity. Infinity is represented by LONG_MAX.
